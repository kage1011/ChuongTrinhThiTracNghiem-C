Cach  thi:

Buoc 1: Dang Nhap

Cuoc 2: Chon Goi cau hoi

Buoc 3: Thuc hien bai thi theo huong dan cua phan mem

Quy Cach Bai Thi:

* moi cau tra loi dung tuong duong :
  + 10 diem doi voi goi 10 cau hoi
  + 5 diem doi voi goi 20 cau hoi
* so diem toi thieu de qua bai thi la: 50/100
